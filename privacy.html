Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Add Image to PDF Chrome extension ("the extension", "we", "our") handles information. We've designed the extension around a simple principle: your files stay on your device.

What the extension does with your files

The extension processes PDF files and images entirely locally inside your browser. PDFs you open, images you add as stamps or signatures, and the resulting output files are never uploaded, transmitted, stored remotely, or shared with us or any third party. Everything happens on your computer.

Information we collect

The extension itself does not collect any personal information. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry inside the extension. We do not have user accounts, and we don't see your files, your edits, or your activity.

Information collected by the website

When you visit our website (welcome page, uninstall page, privacy policy), your browser sends standard request information to GitHub Pages, our hosting provider. This may include your IP address, browser type, and the page requested. We don't operate this logging directly; please review GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Feedback you choose to send us

If you fill out the uninstall feedback form, we receive only what you submit: the reason you selected, any optional comments, and an optional email address. We use this information solely to improve the extension and, if you provided an email, to reply to you. We never sell or share this information.

Permissions used by the extension

The extension requests only the permissions it needs to function:

  • activeTab / scripting: to run the editor interface inside your browser tab.
  • storage: to remember your settings (such as default stamp position and size) locally on your device.

The extension does not request access to your browsing history, bookmarks, downloads, or activity on other websites.

Third-party services

The extension itself does not use any third-party services. The uninstall feedback form on this website uses Formspree to deliver submissions to us. If you submit the form, the contents you provided are processed by Formspree according to their privacy policy.

Children's privacy

The extension is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll change the "Last updated" date above and publish the new version on this page.
